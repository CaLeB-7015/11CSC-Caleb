Sea Level Rise

About

Sea level rise happens when oceans increase in height due to melting ice and warming water. Human activities such as burning fossil fuels release greenhouse gases that contribute to global warming. The effects of sea level rise include: Coastal flooding, Beach erosion, Damage to homes and infrastructure, Loss of habitats for wildlife and Threats to communities near the coast Understanding this issue is important because it affects millions of people worldwide and will continue to impact future generations.
